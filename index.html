NCISP is a free Application for all NCI College Students. The app is a one stop place that intergrates moodle + student portal + NCISU + Academic Calander and many more features.

Whether you're a student looking to study or get tips for your exam's, Enjoy the social life that NCISU brings, or if you want to view your accademic and social calendar, NCISP is ideal for you!

Our App implements all aspects of NCI into one app, giving student's an opportunity to experience NCI academically and socially easier with a click of a button.

NCISP - Homepage

Students can login via their own Student Login ID's or through Facebook. Once logged in, you will be redirected to a menu where you can navigate around the entire app. There is a selection of six different categories: Moodle, NCISU, NCI Website, ExamBuddy, StudentPortal & Help.
